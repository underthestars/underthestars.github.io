WEDDINGS

Add a little extra magic to your wedding reception with a drop-in stargazing lounge under the incredible skies of Wairarapa Dark Sky Reserve. Think bean bags and reclining camp chairs, warm blankets, a large telescope, a friendly guide and the starry sky above.
