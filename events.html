Events

Sometimes we partner with other Wairarapa businesses to run events at local venues. If we have any events scheduled, you can find them here.

Lavender Stargazing 2025/26

We are running our popular events at Ranui Essentials lavender farm again for 2025/26, combining stargazing with the beautiful atmosphere of the lavender gardens overlooking the picturesque plains of Wairarapa towards the Remutaka ranges.

There are two formats of these events on different dates. 4 dates are ticketed and limited to 20 guests. This includes a guided tour of the lavender farm and produce, followed by a stargazing presentation with telescope viewing. https://www.eventfinda.co.nz/2025/private-martinborough-star-gazing-lavender-nights/martinborough

3 dates are open to up to 200 guests at a time to wander the gardens, set up a picnic and chat to Under the Stars who'll be present with telescopes and laser pointers to identify night sky objects, constellations and more https://www.eventfinda.co.nz/2025/martinborough-lavender-star-gazing3/martinborough
